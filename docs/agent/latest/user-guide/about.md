#  About akaBot Agent

**The akaBot Agent** is the center of action when it comes to automation and helping your business grow and becoming more successful each day. It enables you to run workflows which are developed in Studio or distributed by akaBot Center.

It can act as the personal assistant of an end-user *(HR Personnel, Call Center Operator or Data Validation Specialist)* and helps them with small day-to-day tasks or specific parts of a process or can perform independently in the background to ease off the repetitive or long running tasks of multiple users and even whole departments.
