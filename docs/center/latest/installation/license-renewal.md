# License Renewal

To ensure uninterrupted operation of akaBot Center, your license must be renewed before it expires. License renewal provides continued access to all features, software updates, and technical support. The renewal process is similar to initial activation and can be performed online or offline.

## Prerequisites

- You must have an active or recently expired akaBot Center installation.
- A new or extended license key must be obtained from the akaBot sales or support team prior to renewal.
- Administrator access to the akaBot Center web portal is required.
- For online renewal, the server must be able to reach the akaBot licensing service over the internet.

## Renewal Steps

1. Log in to the akaBot Center web portal as an administrator.
2. Go to **Administration** > **License Management**.
3. Review the current license status. If the license has expired or is about to expire, a warning banner will be displayed.
4. Click **Renew License**.
5. Enter the new license key provided by the akaBot team.
6. Choose **Online Renewal** or **Offline Renewal** based on your network environment.
7. For online renewal, click **Submit** to send the renewal request to the licensing server.
8. For offline renewal, download the renewal request file, submit it to akaBot support, and upload the renewal response file when received.
9. Confirm that the new expiry date and license terms are correctly reflected on the License Management page.
10. Restart the akaBot Center service to apply the updated license.

## Notes and Warnings

> **Note:** It is recommended to begin the renewal process at least 14 days before your current license expires to avoid any service interruption.

> **Warning:** Once a license expires, features such as adding new robots, modifying schedules, and accessing certain reports will be disabled until the license is renewed.

> **Note:** After a successful renewal, existing automation workflows and robot connections will not be affected. The renewal only updates the license validity period and terms.
