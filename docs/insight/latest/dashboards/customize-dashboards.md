# Customize Dashboards
## Creating New View

To create a new views, take the following steps:

Step 1: Users click on **Edit Dashboard** in any view to access Tableau UI.

![image-20230519090636-1.png](/static/img/image-20230519090636-1.png)

At this point, the screen will be switched to the view configuration screen of the embedded Tableau platform.

![image-20230519091334-2.png](/static/img/image-20230519091334-2.png)

**Note:** The views in akaBot Insights are divided into two types: Worksheets and Dashboards. A dashboard can include one or multiple worksheets depending on the preferences and purposes of the creator.

Step 2.1: To create a new worksheet, users can either click on the **New Worksheet** icon at the bottom of the page, or enter keyboard shortcut Ctrl + Alt + T.

![image-20230519092843-3.png](/static/img/image-20230519092843-3.png)

Or click on **Worksheet**on the menu bar**,** then **New Worksheet**

![image-20230519094334-4.png](/static/img/image-20230519094334-4.png)

Step 2.2: To create a new dashboard, users click on the **New Dashboard** icon at the bottom of the page. 

![image-20230519095146-6.png](/static/img/image-20230519095146-6.png)

Or click on **Dashboard** on the menu bar, then **New Dashboard.**

![image-20230519095546-8.png](/static/img/image-20230519095546-8.png)

Step 3: Click on **Publish** to save the new view to the list of views in akaBot Insights.

![image-20230519103017-10.png](/static/img/image-20230519103017-10.png)

For detailed instructions on how to proceed next, we recommend reading Tableau’s guides for creating dashboards, found below.

The following pages help you create dashboards through the Tableau UI:

* [Tableau Learning](https://www.tableau.com/learn/training/20201): It provides users with the fundamental knowledge and how to use Tableau.
* [Tableau Documentation](https://www.tableau.com/support/help?_ga=2.148610935.1319140124.1684467311-897470386.1679390771): It provides official Tableau documentation and instructional materials.
* [Tableau Gallery](https://public.tableau.com/en-us/gallery): The Tableau Gallery is a place where you can learn how others design dashboards. It primarily focuses on learning about ideas, design techniques, and combining different types of charts.
* [Tableau Viz Cookbook](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/josh.weyburne/viz/CookBook/VizCookbook): A compilation of 52 different types of charts ranging from the most basic ones.
* [Tableau Chart Catalog](https://public.tableau.com/views/TheTableauChartCatalog/TableauChartExamples?:showVizHome=no): A compilation of 100 basic to advanced types of charts, including a wide range of creative charts to serve various purposes.

## Duplicating Views

To duplicate a view, take the following steps:

Step 1: Users click on **Edit Dashboard** in any view to access Tableau UI.

Step 2: Right-click on the sheet you want to duplicate, then click **Duplicate.**

![image-20230519110519-11.png](/static/img/image-20230519110519-11.png)

Step 3: Click on **Publish** to save the duplicated sheet to the list of views in akaBot Insights.

## Exporting Views

Users can export the visualization in different formats: as a PDF file, as an image file, as a CSV file, as an Excel file, or as a PowerPoint file.

Step 1: To export a view, click on the **Download** icon

![image-20230519132033-1.png](/static/img/image-20230519132033-1.png)

Step 2: Choose the file format you want to download.

![image-20230519132129-2.png](/static/img/image-20230519132129-2.png)

## Deleting Views

Views that are deleted on the akaBot Insights UI will not be completely removed but will be saved on the Tableau site.

To completely delete a view, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Users click on **Edit Dashboard** in any view to access Tableau UI.

Step 2: Right-click on the sheet you want to duplicate, then click **Delete.**

![image-20230519132810-3.png](/static/img/image-20230519132810-3.png)

## Create and Edit your own dashboards

In addition to using the templates, you can customize your dashboard according to your preferences.

**Note**: To become proficient in creating/editing dashboards, please refer to the [Tableau Documentation](https://help.tableau.com/current/pro/desktop/en-us/gettingstarted_overview.htm) (the embedded platform of akaBot Insight).
