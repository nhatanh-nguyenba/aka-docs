## akaBot default dashboards

When accessing Insights for the first time, the **Favorite Views** tab is displayed along with its corresponding default dashboards: **Workflow**, **Queue**, **Agent, and Business**.

![image-20230413112802-1.png](/static/img/image-20230413112802-1.png)

See [Default dashboard](#) for additional details.

## View Dashboards

Users can see all the available views within the **Views** section.

**Note:** A dashboard consists of multiple different views (the dashboard itself is also considered a view).

The list of views & favorite views can be toggled between two display formats: List view and Grid view.

![image-20230518152203-1.png](/static/img/image-20230518152203-1.png)

To view a specific view, the user can click on the desired view from the list.

![image-20230518152921-2.png](/static/img/image-20230518152921-2.png)

## Add & Remove favorite views

The purpose of having a **Favorite Views** section is to allow users to mark and save important, popular, or frequently used views for quick access in the future. The **Favorite Views** section helps users easily locate and access the views they care about without having to search through the entire list of views.

To move views into **Favorite Views**, users click on the corresponding star icon (with stroke only) in **Views** section.

![image-20230518163133-3.png](/static/img/image-20230518163133-3.png)

To remove views from **Favorite Views**, users click on the corresponding star icon (yellow) in **Favorite Views** & **Views** section.

![image-20230518163722-5.png](/static/img/image-20230518163722-5.png)

## Create and Edit your own dashboards

In addition to using the templates, you can customize your dashboard according to your preferences.

**Note**: To become proficient in creating/editing dashboards, please refer to the [Tableau Documentation](https://help.tableau.com/current/pro/desktop/en-us/gettingstarted_overview.htm) (the embedded platform of akaBot Insight).
