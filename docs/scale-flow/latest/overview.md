# Scale Flow

ScaleFlow helps your business receive customer messages, answer faster with AI, and keep every support task organized in one place.

You do not need technical knowledge to use ScaleFlow. Start with the basic setup, connect the channels your customers already use, prepare simple business information for AI, then let your team manage conversations and tickets from one shared workspace.

## How ScaleFlow works

1. A customer sends a message from Zalo, Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, your website [Chat Widget](./user-guide/channels/chat-widget/setting-up-chat-widget), or another connected channel.
2. The message appears in **Inbox**.
3. **AI Assistant** can answer automatically, suggest a response, summarize the conversation, or ask a human staff member to continue.
4. **[Flows](./user-guide/operations/flow-usage)** can run multi-step automation—for example welcome messages, AI handoff, or waiting for a reply—when you need more than a single AI answer.
5. If the issue needs follow-up, your team creates a **Ticket**.
6. Staff members use **Contacts**, **Tickets**, and **Analytics** to finish the work and improve service quality.

## Best starting path

Follow these pages in order if you are new:

1. [Account Registration](./user-guide/getting-started/account-registration): create your account and sign in.
2. [Channel Integration](./user-guide/channels/channel-integration): connect Zalo OA, LINE, WhatsApp, Messenger, Telegram, [Chat Widget](./user-guide/channels/chat-widget/setting-up-chat-widget), and more.
3. [Knowledge Usage](./user-guide/scaleflow-ai/knowledge-usage): add the information AI should learn from.
4. [AI Agent Usage](./user-guide/scaleflow-ai/ai-agent-usage): create the AI worker that uses your Knowledge.
5. [AI Assistant](./user-guide/scaleflow-ai/ai-assistant): turn on the AI experience inside Inbox.
6. [Inbox Usage](./user-guide/operations/inbox-usage): reply to customers and manage conversations.
7. [Ticket Usage](./user-guide/operations/ticket-usage): track customer issues until they are solved.
8. [Flow Usage](./user-guide/operations/flow-usage): automate multi-step workflows with triggers and deployed runs (optional, after channels and agents are ready).

### Website chat & automation (optional)

- [Chat Widget](./user-guide/channels/chat-widget/setting-up-chat-widget) — embed a chat box on your website; messages arrive in Inbox like other channels.
- [Flows](./user-guide/operations/flow-usage) — design step-by-step automation (greeting, AI agent, conditions, deploy runs).

## Everyday example

A small online shop connects its Zalo OA to ScaleFlow. A customer asks, "Do you have size M in black?" The message appears in Inbox. AI Assistant checks the shop's product Knowledge and replies with the available options. If the customer asks for a refund, AI Assistant creates a Ticket and assigns it to the support team. A staff member reviews the ticket, checks the order, replies to the customer, and marks the ticket as resolved.
