# Account Registration

This guide helps you enter Scale Pay for the first time. Most beginners should use **SSO sign-in** because it is the fastest and easiest option.

SSO means you sign in with an account your company already uses, such as Google or Microsoft. You do not need to create and remember a separate Scale Pay password.

## Step 1: Open the Scale Pay login page

1. Open [Scale Pay](https://app.scalepay.vn) in your browser.
2. If you are not signed in yet, Scale Pay sends you to the login page automatically.
3. Choose the sign-in method that matches your organization.

## Step 2: Choose your sign-in method

Scale Pay supports two common ways to get started. Most beginners should choose SSO if it is available.

### Option 1: Register or sign in with SSO

Choose this option first if you see a company sign-in button such as **Continue with Google**, **Continue with Microsoft**, or another company login.

1. Click the SSO button.
2. Choose or enter your work email account.
3. Approve the sign-in request if your company asks for confirmation.
4. Wait for Scale Pay to open your workspace.

![Sign in with SSO](/static/img/sign-in-sso.png)


Use SSO when:

- Your company already gave you a work email account.
- You want the quickest setup.
- You do not want to manage another password.

Example: A new finance staff joins on Monday morning. Their manager adds them to the company workspace. The staff opens Scale Pay, clicks **Continue with Google**, and reaches the dashboard in about one minute.

### Option 2: Register with the form

Use the registration form if your organization does not use SSO, or if your admin specifically asks you to create a Scale Pay password.

1. Select **Register** or **Create account**.
2. Enter your name and email address.
3. Create a password.
4. Submit the form.

![Account registration form](/static/img/sp_register.png)

If Scale Pay asks you to verify your email:

1. Open the verification email.
2. Click the confirmation link.
3. Return to the login page.

## Step 3: Confirm access to your workspace

After sign-in, check that:

- You can see the main Scale Pay dashboard.
- Your profile or account menu appears in the top-right corner.
- You can open important areas such as **Pipeline setting**, **Invoice**, **Purchase order**, or **Matching sets** depending on your role.

If you can sign in but cannot see a page, it usually means your role does not include that access yet. Ask your admin to update your role in [User Management](../organization/user-management) or [Roles & Permissions](../organization/roles-permission).

## Common issues and quick fixes

### I did not receive verification email

- Check Spam, Promotions, or Junk folders.
- Make sure the email address is typed correctly.
- Try registering again with the correct email.

### I forgot my password

- Use the password reset option on the authentication page.
- If you use SSO, reset the password through your company account instead.

## Security tips

- Do not share your password with anyone.
- Always sign out when using a shared computer.
- Use SSO when available because your company can manage access more safely.
