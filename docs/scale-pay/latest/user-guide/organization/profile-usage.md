# Profile Usage

Your Profile is your personal account information in ScalePay. Keeping it updated helps teammates identify you when conversations, tickets, or internal notes are assigned to you.

## What you can do in Profile

In the **Profile** area, you can:

- View your account information
- Update personal details such as first name and last name
- Update your contact details (for example: phone number)
- Check your current role and account status
- Save changes to keep your profile information up to date

Example: If your display name is clear, teammates can easily assign a customer conversation or ticket to you.

## Open your Profile page

![View profile](/static/img/sp_profile.png)

1. In the top-right corner, click your avatar or account menu.
2. Select **Profile**.
3. Review your profile sections, including:
   - Basic information
   - Contact information
   - Account details

## Update your basic information

![Update profile](/static/img/sp_edit.png)

1. On the **Profile** page, click **Edit** (or the pencil icon) in the basic information section.
2. Update fields such as:
   - First name
   - Last name
3. Click **Save Changes**.

![Dialog update profile](/static/img/sp_edit_box.png)

## Update contact information

1. In the contact section, choose the field you want to update.
2. Enter the new value (for example: phone number).
3. Click **Save Changes**.

## Tips for profile updates

- Use your real name so teammates can identify you easily.
- Keep your phone number updated for faster coordination.
- Save each section after editing to avoid losing changes.
- Refresh the page if your latest updates are not shown immediately.

## Troubleshooting

### I cannot open the Profile page

- Check if your session is still active.
- Sign out and sign in again.
- If the issue continues, contact your admin.

### I can view Profile but cannot edit fields

- Your role may be view-only for certain fields.
- Some fields (such as email) can be read-only by policy.
- Contact your admin if you need edit access.

### My profile changes do not appear

- Wait a few seconds and refresh the page.
- Confirm that **Save Changes** was clicked successfully.
- Try updating again and check for validation messages.
