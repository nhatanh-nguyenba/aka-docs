## 2. What is a “cookie”? 
Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser for various purposes. Cookies are used on almost all websites on the internet and akabotvision.com is no exception. Your browser or device usually allows you to change the settings for the usage of cookies. See your browser or device reference information to understand how to configure your settings. 
 
## 3. What are the types of cookies? 

**Strictly necessary cookies.**

These cookies are essential to enable the core functionality of the website such as security, network management, and accessibility. The necessary cookies allow you to load pages, move around the website, and use its features. You may disable these cookies, but it may affect the website’s functionality. Functionality cookies. These cookies are used to remember your preferences such as your user name, language, or region you are in to provide enhanced and personalized features. These cookies may also be used to autofill a form on a website. 

**Analytical cookies.**

These cookies are used to understand how visitors interact with the website. These cookies help provide information on metrics such as the number of visitors, bounce rate, traffic source, etc. Performance cookies. These cookies are used to understand and analyze the key performance indexes of the website which helps in delivering a better user experience for the visitors. 
 

**Marketing cookies.**

These cookies are used to improve our offers and services to be more relevant to you and match your interests. The cookies can also limit the number of times you can see an advertisement, ensure that the ad is shown properly, and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. 

**First and third-party cookies.**

First-party cookies are those set by a website visited by you directly. Third-party cookies are set by third parties (service providers other than us) domains and websites through a website you visit.
